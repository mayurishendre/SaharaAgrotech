MAXO-CAL
  • Super Saturated Formulation Contain Calcium
  • Key Benefits:-
  • Calcium is an essential nutrient for plants that helps with growth, development, and resistance to disease. Calcium helps strengthen cell walls, improve root growth, and activate enzymes. It also helps plants respond to stress.
  • Cell wall strength:
  • Calcium helps form the compounds that make up cell waths, which strengthens the plant structure.
  • Root growth Calcium helps stimulate root growth, which improves the plant's ability to absorb water, air, and nutrients from the soil.
  • Enzyme activation Calcium activates several plant enzyme systems.
  • Stress response: Calcium helps plants respond to biotic stress such as non-self recognition.
  • Disease resistance:
  • Calcium helps increase a plant's resistance to outside attack
  • Fruit quality:
  • Calcium helps produce firmer fruits with better taste and shelf life.
  • Storage Calcium helps strengthen potato cell walls, which reduces shrink and bacterial soft rot when the potatoes are stored.
  • Soil structure:
  • Calcium can improve soil structure by displacing sodium and allowing clay particles to aggregate. Calcium deficiencies can occur in acidic or sandy soils, where calcium can leach out through rain or irrigation.
